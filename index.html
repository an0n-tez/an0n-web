One Eleven (etc.) by an0n.tez

A (mostly) generative art experiment/periodical.

All work is CC0, which means that it is public domain and you may do what you like with it.

You don't need to attribute us or anyone else, but please tag @an0n_tez on twitter because we're curious.

Feel free to follow that account too, it will be more up-to-date than this website.

There is an SDK/FXHash template available to make it easy to embed One Eleven into a project.
